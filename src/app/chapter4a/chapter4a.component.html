Chapter 4

Concrete Jungle

You decide better safe than sorry so you assault a nearby mormon and steal their clothes and head into the city. You meet a young Chinietnamese Man named Brother Du. He tells you of his homeland of Kansas City before he became Mormonese. He agrees to accompany you there.

You make it Kansas City, but the Cascadians are there waiting for you. In an act of ultimate selflessness, Brother Du sacrifices himself so that you can escape. You board a zeppelin bound for New York

You arrive in New York, the melting pot of the world. You easily disappear there and live out your days as a street hot dog vendor. It's not the best life, but you never have to see a tree again!
